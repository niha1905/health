Blood Pressure

Low : Systolic Blood Pressure Below 90 mmHg and Diastolic Blood Pressure Below 60 mmHg

Normal : Systolic Blood Pressure Around 90 mmHg to 120 mmHg and Diastolic Blood Pressure Around 60 mmHg to 80 mmHg

High : Systolic Blood Pressure 130 mmHg and above and Diastolic Blood Pressure 80 mmHg and above (Hypertension)

Blood Pressure

Blood pressure measures the force of blood against artery walls. Low pressure may lead to dizziness; high pressure can cause health risks.
